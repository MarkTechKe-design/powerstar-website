Who We Are

Powerstar Supermarkets is more than just a retail outlet; we are a community partner. Born from a desire to bring high-quality goods at honest prices to Kenyan neighborhoods, we have grown into a trusted household name.

We believe that shopping should be dignified, easy, and affordable. Our "Save Everyday" philosophy isn't just a slogan—it is the operational backbone of our procurement, logistics, and service delivery.
