Who We Are

Independent. Innovative. Honest.

Welcome to Powerstar

Powerstar Supermarkets is an independent, innovative, and honest sustainable outlet dedicated to providing the most inviting buying environment in the industry. We offer customers a wide range of goods while helping them save time and money every day.

Our Mission

To be an independent, innovative, honest, and sustainable outlet in which customers are able to choose from a wide range of goods and offering the most inviting buying environment in the industry while saving the customer time and money everyday.

Our Vision

To deliver an exceptional shopping experience by offering the best service, value, quality, and freshest products while being good stewards of our environment and giving back to the community we serve.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide us everyday.

Teamwork Meeting our challenges and opportunities as one team and focused on common goals.
Respect Treating our colleagues, customers, suppliers, and venders with respect and dignity.
Excellence Striving for excellence and working to improve everyday.
Accountability Holding ourselves accountable for delivering results and doing the right thing everyday.
Passion Offering our customers the best shopping experience by exceeding their expectations.
