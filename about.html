About Powerstar Supermarkets

Powerstar Supermarkets is an independent, innovative, and honest sustainable outlet dedicated to providing the most inviting buying environment in the industry. We offer customers a wide range of goods while helping them save time and money every day. Our team is committed to excellence, quality service, and delivering the freshest products to ensure an exceptional shopping experience for every customer.

Our Mission

To be an independent, innovative, honest, and sustainable outlet where customers can choose from a wide range of goods while enjoying the most inviting buying environment in the industry — all while saving time and money every day.

Our Vision

To deliver an exceptional shopping experience by offering the best service, value, quality, and freshest products while being responsible stewards of our environment and giving back to the communities we proudly serve.

Our Core Values

  • Teamwork: Meeting challenges and opportunities as one team aligned toward common goals.
  • Respect: Treating colleagues, customers, suppliers, and vendors with dignity and respect.
  • Excellence: Striving for excellence and working to improve every day.
  • Accountability: Holding ourselves responsible for delivering results and doing the right thing — every day.
  • Passion: Exceeding customer expectations and delivering the best shopping experience.
