Who We Are

Powerstar Supermarkets is more than a retail outlet; it is a long-term community partner committed to improving everyday living within the neighborhoods we serve. Established with a clear objective to provide consistently high-quality products at fair and transparent prices, Powerstar has steadily evolved into a trusted household brand across Kenya.

Our operations are guided by the belief that shopping should be dignified, convenient, and accessible to all. This philosophy is embedded in our “Save Everyday” commitment, which goes beyond messaging to form the foundation of our business model. It informs our procurement strategy, supply chain efficiency, pricing discipline, and customer service standards—ensuring value is delivered consistently, sustainably, and at scale.
