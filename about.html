About Powerstar Supermarkets

Powerstar Supermarkets is an independent, innovative, and honest sustainable retail outlet dedicated to offering the most inviting buying environment in the industry. Our mission is to provide customers with a wide range of goods while saving them time and money every day. We strive to deliver an exceptional shopping experience by offering the best service, value, quality, and the freshest products. Beyond retail, we are committed to being responsible stewards of our environment and giving back to the communities we serve. Powerstar...save everyday!

Our Mission

To be an independent, innovative, honest, and sustainable outlet that offers customers a wide range of goods in the most inviting buying environment in the industry—while helping them save time and money every day.

Our Vision

To deliver an exceptional shopping experience by offering the best service, value, quality, and freshest products—while being good stewards of our environment and giving back to the community we serve.

Our Core Values

  • Teamwork – Meeting our challenges and opportunities as one team, focused on common goals.
  • Respect – Treating our colleagues, customers, suppliers, and vendors with dignity and respect.
  • Excellence – Striving for excellence and working to improve every day.
  • Accountability – Holding ourselves accountable for delivering results and doing the right thing every day.
  • Passion – Exceeding customer expectations by offering the best shopping experience.
