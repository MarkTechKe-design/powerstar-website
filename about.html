About Powerstar Supermarkets

Powerstar Supermarkets is a leading retail chain with 11 branches across Kenya. We focus on quality products, excellent customer service, and community engagement. Our mission is to provide affordable and fresh products for families while creating a reliable shopping experience.

We are constantly expanding, opening new branches, and innovating to meet customer needs. Our values include integrity, sustainability, and commitment to excellence.
